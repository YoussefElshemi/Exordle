How To Play


Guess the EXORDLE.

Each guess must be a valid word or a place on campus. Hit the enter button to submit.

After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

The objetive of the game is to guess locations on campus. To obtain points you must check in at the correct location.

The letter C is in the word and in the correct spot.
The letter O is in the word but in the correct spot.
The letter E is not in the word in any spot.
